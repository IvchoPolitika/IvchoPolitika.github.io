Chapter 1: The Beginning

Eons ago, in ancient times, Sensibilia was known as a stronghold of wisdom and magic. The city rose in grandeur, surrounded by high mountains and limited only by the mind and will of its inhabitants. But over time, knowledge of a mysterious artifact began to circulate among the legends of the people.

This artifact was called the "Starfire" and was believed to be the possession of the ancient gods. According to the myths, it was the key to immeasurable magical power that could change the fate of worlds. But through time, the knowledge of the Starfire's whereabouts faded and the legends were forgotten.

Sensibilia, the city of great sages and sorcerers, has always been obsessed with the mysteries of the past and the search for the secrets of the universe. In the heart of this majestic city that rises above the valley, where magic is in every stone and in the air, Alicia begins her journey.

She treads the streets filled with the scent of magical merchant stands and crowds of people who move with the same purpose as her - seeking the truth and power of the Starfire. Alicia radiates strength and determination, as she makes her way through the city's many alleys and plazas. Her eyes are fiery and deep, reflecting her inner fire and confidence in her path.
